Features

CarrierScope offers a suite of intelligent tools designed to help logistics professionals make data-driven decisions and build stronger partnerships across the U.S. freight network.

  • Ratings & Reviews: Evaluate brokers and carriers based on verified performance data, customer feedback, and delivery metrics.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Access real-time insights into reliability scores, on-time delivery rates, and service trends.
  • Reputation Tracking: Monitor partner reputation and service consistency over time to identify top performers.
  • Quality Alerts: Get notified when broker or carrier ratings drop below your thresholds or show irregular activity.
  • Custom Reports: Generate detailed visual analytics to support audits, compliance reviews, and management reporting.

Every feature is built to simplify logistics management and improve trust between shippers, brokers, and carriers.
